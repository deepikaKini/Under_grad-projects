Cycling is not just a hobby. It is a passion. A passion that is unmatched by any other sport or activity. It is something that you can do alone, do it in groups, do it for leisure, do it as a sport, be competitive, cover short distances, go for long rides and the list goes on. When it is a team activity it fosters team spirit and builds the bonds strongly. When you are alone at it, it gives you immense relaxation and calms your mind. Montra understands this spirt and conducts events in all cities called ‘Gran Fondo’. It brings together cycle enthusiasts and furthers the belief of ‘Ride. Believe’.
